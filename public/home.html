Resource Gathering: To build a base in Rust, players must gather various resources such as wood, stone, and metal. These materials are used to craft different components of the base.

-

Building Materials: Rust features different building materials like wood, stone, and metal. Wood is the weakest, while metal and armored structures offer the best protection.

-

Base Components: Players can build different blocks such as walls, roofs, doors, and windows to create their base. Foundations are necessary for structure stability.

-

Raiding and Defense: Bases can be raided by other players using explosives or other tools. To protect against this, players often design their bases with reinforced walls and hidden rooms.

-

Tool Cupboard (TC): The TC is used to claim land and prevent other players from building or destroying parts of the base within a certain radius. It also allows players to manage their base's privileges.
